DIGITAL BUSINESS CARD PRO — Sales Copy (Gumroad / Selar / Payhip)

One-line pitch
-------------
A clean, mobile-first digital business card you can share as a link or QR code — with a built-in contact download (.VCF) and optional no-code Edit Mode.

What buyers get
--------------
- A ready-to-use digital business card (single-page website)
- Light/Dark theme toggle
- Tap-to-call, email, website, WhatsApp, and social buttons
- QR code that automatically points to the live card URL
- "Save Contact" button that downloads a vCard (.VCF) instantly
- Share button (Web Share API + clipboard fallback)
- Quick Edit panel (no coding) + a Lock switch to disable edits when done

Who it's perfect for
--------------------
Freelancers, small business owners, job seekers, creators, realtors, barbers/salon owners, photographers, consultants, coaches, and anyone who wants a premium online card.

How it works (3 steps)
----------------------
1) Open the file and click Edit
2) Add your details and Save
3) Upload the folder to any host (GitHub Pages / Netlify / Vercel) and share the link or QR

Why it’s better than a normal business card
-------------------------------------------
- One link replaces a stack of paper cards
- People can save your contact in 1 tap
- QR works instantly at events and meetings
- Looks premium on any phone
